Mario Kart is a colorful racing game series set in the whimsical world of the Mushroom Kingdom. Players race as iconic Nintendo characters, each with unique karts and abilities. Tracks are filled with imaginative landscapes, from lush forests to space stations, and loaded with hazards. Power-ups like shells, bananas, and speed boosts add chaos and strategy to the competition. The goal is simple: cross the finish line first while dodging traps and outsmarting rivals.
